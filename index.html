Twitch Clip Shoutout can play clips of streamers while you are live.

It works through a webpage loaded into your scene in your streaming application.

It monitors your twitch chat for the command !so and will play a random clip from that streamer.

Created using ComfyJS and Twitch API.



Finding clips by you might cause a slight delay
